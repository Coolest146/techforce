STORY
As the Founder and CEO, I bring a strong background in mechatronics engineering, robotics research, and real-world automation projects. From building robotic arms and AI-driven systems to launching national tech expos, I’ve always believed in learning by doing. MAS-MAX isn’t just me—it’s a team of dedicated engineers, innovators, and educators committed to making robotics and automation a hands-on reality. Our strength lies in combining technical expertise with community-based problem-solving, which is why we’re trusted by schools, businesses, and research labs alike.
JOURNEY
We started with basic tools, limited space, and a big vision. At the beginning, we faced challenges like sourcing parts, gaining trust, and convincing people that local tech could be just as powerful as imported systems. But we never stopped. From our humble beginnings, we’ve now established a fully equipped mechatronics lab, launched working prototypes (from harvesting robots to 6-axis robotic arms), and introduced programs that train young talents in real industrial skills. Today, MAS-MAX is expanding into product development, automation services, training programs, and community tech events, including the well-received Boolean Robotics & RC Racing Expo.
VISION
To become Africa’s leading force in robotics innovation—shaping the future of automation, education, and industrial growth across the continent.
MISSION
To develop affordable, intelligent, and locally-built robotic and automation systems that empower industries, students, and innovators.
